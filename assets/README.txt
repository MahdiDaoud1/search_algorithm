Place PNG icons here (recommended size: 64x64 or 128x128, transparent background).

Required filenames:
  spike.png    — Spike trap    (red skull or danger icon)
  freeze.png   — Freeze zone   (snowflake or ice icon)
  vortex.png   — Vortex trap   (spiral or portal icon)
  bomb.png     — Bomb bonus    (explosion or bomb icon)
  speed.png    — Speed boost   (lightning bolt icon)
  coin.png     — Gold coin     (coin or gem icon)

Suggested search on Flaticon:
  "skull"        → spike.png
  "snowflake"    → freeze.png
  "vortex"       → vortex.png
  "bomb"         → bomb.png
  "lightning"    → speed.png
  "coin"         → coin.png

If a file is missing the tile still renders with its background colour.
